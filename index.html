Her Story

The Life of a Remarkable Woman

Ruth Omolola Bayode · Born to Shine

Academic & Professional Foundations

Ruth's journey is rooted in a deep-seated passion for understanding the natural world. A scholar at heart, she has dedicated herself to the fields of Biology and Science Education — where she doesn't just study life, she seeks to improve the quality of it. As a skilled Agripreneur, she has been a driving force behind Fresh Catch Farms Ltd, bringing technical precision to the world of aquaculture and sustainable farming.

A Vision for the Nation

Beyond the laboratory and the farm, Ruth carries a heavy burden for the future of our nation. She is a woman of vision who aspires to influence the very corridors of the Ministry of Education. Her goal is clear: to impact the Nigerian educational system by fostering scientific literacy and empowering the next generation of thinkers. She believes that education is the most powerful tool for change, and she lives that belief every single day.

Faith & Mentorship

At the core of Ruth's life is her unwavering Christian faith. It is the compass that guides her actions and the foundation of her character. Alongside her husband, she has been a vital part of mentorship initiatives like the "Choice Architects" platform, providing teenagers and young adults with the framework to make sound, God-honoring life decisions. Whether she is advocating for sexual purity or providing guidance to parents, her voice is one of wisdom, compassion, and truth.

The Heart of the Home

To her husband Emmanuel, Ruth is more than a partner — she is a confidante, a co-builder of dreams, and his greatest support system. Their union, celebrated on November 8, 2025, marked the beginning of a powerful partnership that blends geospatial science with agricultural innovation and ministry. In the kitchen, Ruth's creativity shines through her passion for the culinary arts, seeing cooking as an avenue for financial independence and a way to bring joy to those around her.
